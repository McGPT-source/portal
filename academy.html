CORE Sales

Great discovery focuses on the "Why buy?"

Phase 1: Discovery & Qualification

This initial phase is about deep listening and understanding. We use tools like the Value Mapper to connect a prospect's challenges to our core strengths. The goal is not to sell, but to diagnose. By the end of this phase, we should have a clear understanding of the customer's pain and the potential metrics for success.

To learn more visit this module on Sales Academy (360Learning).

Phase 2: Building the Business Case

Once a legitimate pain is identified, we transition to quantifying the value of our solution. This involves co-creating a business case with our champion, aligning our technical solution to measurable business outcomes. The MEDDICC Scorecard becomes critical here to track our alignment with the key people and processes involved in the decision.

To learn more visit this module on Sales Academy (360Learning).

Phase 3: Evaluation & Justification

In this phase, we prove our claims through a PoC or detailed evaluation. Success is not just technical validation, but demonstrating the agreed-upon business value. We focus on differentiating against the competition and mitigating any perceived risks or blockers, ensuring our champion is equipped to justify the investment to the Economic Buyer.

To learn more visit this module on Sales Academy (360Learning).

STRATEGIC Sales

Compelling business cases answer "Why now?"

1. Map Stakeholders to Find Your Mobilizer

Before you can create urgency, you must understand who holds the power to act. Use tools like Sales Navigator & Generative AI to identify stakeholders and the Economic Buyer. Tailor your message for different personas (Strategic, Tactical, Operational) to build consensus and find a champion who can drive change.

To learn more visit this module on Sales Academy (360Learning).

2. Master Meetings & Demos to Drive Momentum

Every interaction is an opportunity to move the deal forward. Prepare with a clear agenda, execute by focusing on their pain points, and **follow up** immediately with a summary and next steps to maintain the urgency you've built.

To learn more visit this module on Sales Academy (360Learning).

3. Qualify with MEDDICC to Build the Business Case

The MEDDICC Scorecard is your tool for rigorously qualifying a deal. Use it to ask targeted questions about Metrics, Decision Criteria, and the Decision Process. By quantifying their pain and understanding their timeline, you can align Scaleway’s value directly with their most pressing needs.

To learn more visit this module on Sales Academy (360Learning).

4. Accelerate Deal Progression with Trigger Events

Leverage internal or external pressures to accelerate the decision. Use ROI and Case Studies to turn your solution into a "must-have." Listen for trigger events like regulatory changes, security vulnerabilities, or budget cycles to provide the final, powerful answer to "Why Now?".

To learn more visit this module on Sales Academy (360Learning).

ELITE Sales

Justify Scaleway's unique differentiation.

Solution Architects as Partners

Our Pre-Sales and Solution Architects (SAs) are your technical partners, ensuring every solution is tailored, validated, and optimized for your unique needs.

  • Expertise on Demand: SAs dedicate their time to customer meetings, technical Q&A, and building custom demos.
  • Early Engagement: Involve SAs from qualification to closing to ensure technical feasibility and alignment.
  • Seamless Collaboration: Use dedicated channels like #sales-technical-questions for rapid, expert support.
